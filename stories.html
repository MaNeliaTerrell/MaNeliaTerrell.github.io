HOW I SEE MY FUTURE

by Ma. Nelia Terrell





I discovered my interest in technology when I worked as technical support for a local telecommunications company in Mississippi few years ago. I realized how I have enjoyed troubleshooting as challenges pushes me to do better. I also enjoyed designing - I make birthday and small wedding invitations from scratch and create online invitations using my own designs.

It is because of these reasons that I decided to give Information/Software Technology attention. I was the tech-person at home. I setup everyone's phones, laptops, tablets and desktops and my in-laws would also call me for help. I believe that this is a promising and rewarding career, and something that I will definitely enjoy.
