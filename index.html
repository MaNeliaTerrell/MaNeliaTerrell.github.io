About Me

by Ma. Nelia Terrell





Hello! My name is Ma. Nelia Terrell. A lot of people get confused with my name. I go by Nelia. The "Ma." part of my name is actually a Spanish abbreviation for Maria. Therefore, my complete name is read as Maria Nelia. I was born and raised in the Philippines. I was hired to teach in Fayette County, Georgia in 2005. I met my husband in 2006 and started a family a year after. We are blessed with 2 children - our first-born is now in 7th Grade and our younger son is in 3rd Grade. We currently reside in McDonough, Georgia.

I have been teaching for 14 years when I decided to stop after our son was born to focus on our family. I worked from home as a customer service representative at some point and worked as an online scorer for 3 different scoring companies, scoring state assessments for Missouri, Maryland, Pennsylvania and California.
